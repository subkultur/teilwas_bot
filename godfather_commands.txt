cancel - Cancel current action (alias: c)
add - Add a new entry (alias: a)
search - Search other users' entries (alias: s)
list - List own entries (alias: l)
delete - Delete one of your entries (alias: d)
subscribe -  Add a new subscription (alias: sub, add_subscription, as)
list_subscriptions - List own subscriptions (alias: ls)
delete_subscription - Delete one of your subscriptions (alias: ds)
